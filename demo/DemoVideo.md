## What This Demo Covers

The demonstration walks through the complete ExactPay experience, including:

- The challenges faced by global migrant workers.
- Financial certainty through upfront recipient amount calculation.
- Verify Once, Reuse Anywhere using Zero-Knowledge verification.
- Offline-first payment preparation for low-connectivity environments.
- Portable Financial Profile for future lending and insurance.
- High-level technical workflow built on Stellar.

## Demo Duration

Approximately 4 minutes 20 seconds.

## Objective

This demo illustrates how ExactPay transforms cross-border remittances from uncertain transactions into predictable financial experiences by combining modern blockchain infrastructure with portable digital identity.
