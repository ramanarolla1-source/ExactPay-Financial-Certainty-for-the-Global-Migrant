ExactPay
Financial Certainty for the Global Migrant

Know Exactly What Reaches Home.

Why ExactPay Exists

More than 280 million international migrants support families across borders through monthly remittances.

For them, every transfer represents much more than money.

It pays for school fees, medicines, groceries, rent, and the everyday needs of the people they love.

Yet today's cross-border payment experience remains filled with uncertainty.

Workers often don't know:

Exactly how much money will reach home.
How much will be lost to hidden exchange rate spreads and intermediary fees.
Why they must repeatedly verify their identity across financial services.
Whether poor internet connectivity will prevent them from sending money when it matters most.

The world doesn't need another remittance application.

It needs financial certainty.

The Problem

Current cross-border payment systems create four fundamental challenges.

Hidden Financial Uncertainty

Exchange rates fluctuate.

Intermediary fees vary.

Settlement paths differ.

As a result, senders often discover the final received amount only after the payment is completed.

Repeated Identity Verification

Users repeatedly submit the same identity documents every time they access new financial services.

The verification process becomes repetitive, expensive and frustrating.

Connectivity Constraints

Many migrant workers live and work in mines, plantations, construction sites and remote industrial areas where reliable internet connectivity cannot be assumed.

Financial support should never depend entirely on network availability.

Financial History That Goes Nowhere

Every successful payment demonstrates financial responsibility.

Yet today's remittance history rarely contributes toward future access to lending, insurance or other financial opportunities.

Our Solution

ExactPay introduces a new principle:

Financial Certainty.

Instead of asking users to simply send money faster,

ExactPay helps them send money with confidence.

Core Capabilities
Know Exactly What Reaches Home

Before a payment begins, ExactPay determines:

Settlement path
Exchange rate
Network costs
Processing fees

allowing the sender to know the exact amount the recipient will receive before confirming the transaction.

Verify Once. Reuse Anywhere.

ExactPay transforms traditional identity verification into a reusable digital credential.

Using Zero-Knowledge verification techniques, users verify themselves once while preserving privacy.

The resulting credential can be securely reused across compatible financial services without repeatedly exposing sensitive personal information.

Offline-First Transaction Experience

Financial inclusion should not stop where mobile coverage ends.

ExactPay explores an offline-first architecture that allows users to prepare and authorize transactions without internet connectivity.

Once connectivity becomes available through trusted relay mechanisms, transactions synchronize securely with the network.

Portable Financial Profile

Every successful transaction contributes toward a portable financial profile.

Instead of ending as isolated payment history,

verified financial behaviour becomes a reusable asset that can support:

Micro-lending
Insurance
Responsible credit assessment
Additional financial services

Our philosophy is simple:

Hide blockchain. Expose certainty.

Why Blockchain?

Cross-border payments involve multiple intermediaries, fragmented verification processes and settlement uncertainty.

Blockchain enables programmable settlement while cryptographic verification enables portable identity.

Together, they allow financial experiences that traditional infrastructure struggles to provide efficiently.

Technology remains invisible.

The user experiences only certainty.
